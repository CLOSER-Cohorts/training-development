##  Aim of Foundation course

Now we have covered the essentials for using and understanding metadata including the benefits to yourself, others and the wider community, the Foundation course will build on this and will introduce additional foundational metadata principles. These are key to understanding how to manage metadata and data, before going on to how to make decisions about what and how to create metadata. Finally, we will look at how to create metadata for your own research.

- List of units/topics

We will look at creating metadata predominantly for quantitative, tabular data (data found in tables). We will also look at how you can create metadata for data collection instruments such as questionnaires and surveys.
